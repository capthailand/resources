# Center for Asylum Protection (CAP) — Branding Guidelines

This document outlines the standard formatting and branding conventions for CAP documents. These guidelines are intended to ensure consistency, clarity, and a professional presentation across all materials.

---

## Documents

The properties below are defined in styles in templates. Do not set these properties manually if a style exists!

### Sheet Size and Layout

- **Format:** A4  
- **Margins:**  
  - Top: 2 cm  
  - Bottom: 1.5 cm  
  - Left: 2 cm  
  - Right: 2 cm  

- **Header:**  
  - Positioned 0.5 cm from the top  
  - Height: 2 cm  
  - Logo: Left-aligned, 7.5 × 1.55 cm  

- **Footer:**  
  - Height: 1 cm (for single-line footers)  
  - Page numbers: Right-aligned  

- **Sectioning:**  
  - Heading 1 typically begins on a new page  



## Typography

**Base Font Family:** IBM Plex Sans  
- Google Fonts: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Sans  
- Bunny Fonts: https://fonts.bunny.net/family/ibm-plex-sans  

**Monospace Font:** IBM Plex Mono (or IBM Plex Mono Light)



### Text Styles

**Title**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Semibold  
- Size: 28 (adjustable depending on context)  
- Colour: Black or primary accent  
- Spacing: 2 cm+ above, 0.14 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.0  

**Subtitle**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Light  
- Size: 20  
- Colour: #808080  
- Spacing: 0 cm above, 0.35 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  

**Heading 1**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Semibold  
- Size: 24  
- Colour: Black or primary accent  
- Spacing: 2 cm above, 0.14 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  

**Heading 2**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Light  
- Size: 18  
- Colour: Black or primary accent  
- Spacing: 0.56 cm above, 0.14 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  

**Heading 3**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Semibold  
- Size: 14  
- Colour: Black or primary accent  
- Spacing: 0.28 cm above, 0.14 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  

**Heading 4**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Semibold  
- Size: 12  
- Colour: Black or primary accent  
- Spacing: 0.14 cm above, 0.07 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  

**Body Text (Normal)**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Light  
- Size: 10  
- Colour: Black
- Spacing: 0.0 cm above, 0.35 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  

**Emphasis**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Semibold  
- Size: 10  

**Code / Tables / Monospace**  
- Font: IBM Plex Mono or IBM Plex Mono Light  

**Quotes**  
- Font: IBM Plex Sans Light  
- Size: 10  
- Colour: Black  
- Spacing: 0 cm above, 0.35 cm below  
- Line spacing: 1.15  
- Indentation: 1 cm (left and right)  
- Left border: 2.25 pt (accent colour)  
- Padding: 0.28 cm on all sides  
- Optional: Muted accent background, with optional 0.5 pt accent border  


## Dividers

- Style: Bottom border  
- Colour: Black or primary accent  
- Thickness: 0.5 pt or 2.25 pt  



## Colours

Define a consistent and restrained palette for all CAP materials:

### Core Brand Colours

- **Primary Accent:** `#29307d`  
  A deep indigo-blue that conveys trust, authority, and institutional credibility.

- **Muted Primary Accent:** `#e6e8f5`  
  A soft, desaturated tint of the primary colour for subtle backgrounds and callouts.

- **Secondary Accent:** `#f9ad18`  
  A warm amber tone used for emphasis, highlights, and secondary visual elements.

- **Muted Secondary Accent:** `#fff2d6`  
  A light amber tint suitable for background fills and low-contrast emphasis.



### Neutral Colours

- **Gray text on white background:** `#808080`  
- **Light grey background:** `#ededed`  
- **Dark background:** `#14171c`  



### Semantic Colours

Used for UI-like elements, callouts, or informational boxes:

- **Information (Blue):**  
  - Text: `#085ec7`  
  - Background: `#eef5fc`  

- **Success (Green):**  
  - Text: `#107c10`  
  - Background: `#dff6dd`  

- **Danger (Red):**  
  - Text: `#bc2f32`  
  - Background: `#f6a8a8`  

- **Warning (Amber):**  
  - Text: `#986f0b`  
  - Background: `#fff4ce`  



### Usage Guidance

- Use the **primary accent** for headings, dividers, and key structural elements.  
- Use the **secondary accent** sparingly to draw attention or highlight important content.  
- Use **muted variants** for backgrounds, quote blocks, and low-emphasis sections.  
- Neutral colours should dominate body content to maintain readability and restraint.  
- Semantic colours should be used consistently and only for their intended meaning.



## Notes

- Font sizes may be adjusted depending on document type (e.g., cover pages vs. internal reports).  
- Maintain consistent spacing, hierarchy, and alignment across all documents.  
- Avoid unnecessary variation in fonts, colours, or layout beyond what is defined here.  
- Consistency should take precedence over stylistic preference.

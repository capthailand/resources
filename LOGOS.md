# Logos

## Center for Asylum Protection

> **IN BRIEF**  
> Use [CAP-logo.png](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/CAP-logo.png) by default, unless a specific exception applies.

Use the CAP logo in all materials where it can be displayed clearly and at an appropriate size. At a minimum, the text `PEOPLE SERVING PEOPLE FOUNDATION / CENTER FOR ASYLUM PROTECTION` must remain legible.

**General Use**  
Unless there is a clear reason to choose otherwise, default to [CAP-logo.png](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/CAP-logo.png) as the organization’s primary logo.

**Black and White**  
Use the [black-and-white version](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/CAP-logo-BW.png) when materials are intended for black-and-white or grayscale presentation. Do not convert the standard logo to grayscale—always use the designated black-and-white file.

**Thai / Thai Black and White**  
Thai-language versions of the logo are available [here](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/CAP-logo-Thai.png), including a [black-and-white version](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/CAP-logo-Thai-BW.png). These should be used only when the audience is exclusively Thai. Note that the language of the content alone is not the deciding factor—materials such as public-facing NGO reports may still require the standard (non-Thai) logo if the audience is broader.

## People Serving People Foundation

This repository also includes logos for the People Serving People Foundation (PSPF). The same usage guidelines outlined above for CAP logos apply here. Note that the PSPF logo is presented in a dual-language format.
- [Colour](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/PSPF-logo.png)
- [Black-and-white](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/PSPF-logo-BW.png)

## Logomark

The logomark consists of two stylized people people holding a flame between them. The logomark should be used where the full CAP or PSPF logo would not be legible, such as: 
- Online profile pictures
- Small-size renderings in documents or on the web
- As signposting
- On specific documents as directed by the office.
 
Please use the [colour](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/Logomark.png) version unless your material is [black-and-white or grayscale](https://github.com/capthailand/resources/blob/main/logos/Logomark-BW.png).
